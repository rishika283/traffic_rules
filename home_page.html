TRAFFIC RULES

We observe a number of accidents taking place each day because of irresponsible driving. It is the duty of every responsible citizen to follow traffic rules when on a road. These rules are essential for road safety. Obeying traffic rules helps avoid accidents and mishaps on the road. It helps save lives.


    BASIC RULES TO BE FOLLOWED ON THE ROAD
  • Cross road using the zebra-crossing.
  • You must have a valid driving license to drive a vehicle.
  • Wear seatbelt while in a car.
  • Wear a helmet when on a two-wheeler.
